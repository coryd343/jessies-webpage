I'm Jessie, and I've been an artist my whole life.

My inspiration is equal parts fantasy and nature. I see these as the invention of man and the invention of God and I often represent both in my creations.

I strive to let my art drive the storytelling in my childrens' books. I create digital illustrations as well as write my own narratives and poems. I also sew custom hats. I work on commission for both the illustrations and hats.
